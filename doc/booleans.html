Booleans

;https://github.com/viebel/lambda-calculus (ns demo.lambda (:refer-clojure :exclude [not and or]) (:require-macros [lambda-calculus.macros :refer [lambda-bool]] [gadjett.macros :refer [disp]]) (:require [lambda-calculus.booleans :refer [T F negation not conjunction and disjunction or]]))

A boolean n is viewed as either T or F:

[T F T T F]

Comparison

(= T T)
(= T F)

Negation a.k.a not

You can use either negation or not.
Here is the truth table:
(disp (negation T) (negation F))

(disp (not T) (not F))

Conjunction a.k.a and

You can use either conjunction or and.
Here is the truth table:
(disp ((conjunction T) T) ((conjunction T) F) ((conjunction F) F) ((conjunction F) T))

(disp ((and T) T) ((and T) F) ((and F) F) ((and F) T))

Disjunction a.k.a or

You can use either disjunction or or.
Here is the truth table:
(disp ((disjunction T) T) ((disjunction T) F) ((disjunction F) F) ((disjunction F) T))

(disp ((or T) T) ((or T) F) ((or F) F) ((or F) T))
